Who We Are

Hagios Consulting Limited is a multidisciplinary consulting firm operating within Malawi’s construction industry. Since our establishment in 2016, we have provided professional services in project management, construction supervision, structural and highway design, and procurement consultancy.

Our highly skilled engineers and project managers combine technical expertise with sound judgment to ensure that every design is safe, practical, durable, and economically efficient — both in initial cost and long-term maintenance.
